# Code Of Conduct

This project welcomes practical, technical feedback from FPV builders,
firmware developers, and hardware reviewers.

## Expected

- Be specific and constructive.
- Separate confirmed issues from guesses.
- Include logs, screenshots, schematic references, or reproduction notes when
  possible.
- Keep safety concerns clear and direct.

## Not Welcome

- Personal attacks, harassment, or insults.
- Unsafe advice presented as proven.
- Spam, fake engagement, or unrelated promotion.

Moderation decisions are made to keep review focused, safe, and useful.
