# Contributing

Thanks for taking a look at SkyPilot H743. The most useful contributions are
specific review notes, comparable design references, and field-test reports.

## Helpful Feedback

- ICM42688P IMU wiring, DRDY interrupt usage, and SPI signal-integrity notes.
- PCB layout, grounding, power filtering, and vibration-isolation review.
- INAV target configuration concerns.
- Build photos, bench notes, and Blackbox logs from comparable STM32H743 boards.

## Safety

Please treat this as an open hardware project under review. Verify schematics,
PCB layout, firmware target configuration, and power connections before flying.
